About Tofunmi Sodimu

Tofunmi Sodimu is a graduate student at Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, obtaining a masters of science degree in Robotics. She graduated with a bachelors of science degree in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Texas at Dallas in May 2022. While obtaining her bachelors degree, she was a student research assistant in 'Ding's Optical Imaging lab' where she and her fellow lab members developed a Light Sheet Fluorescence Microscope (LSFM) for the imaging of biological samples. Tofunmi was also involved in the development of a Bladder Flowscope (patent approved) for volume measurement during certain urological processes. Please see the projects and portfolio sections for more information on Tofunmi's work. Asides from the projects mentioned above, Oluwatofunmi constantly seeks to develop herself by learning new skills and advancing her knowledge in the areas of technology and medicine.
